# SEION Claims Policy

## Allowed

- SEION v0.1 provides numerical ternary-product, projector, associator, closure, commutator, and cyclic-defect audits.
- SEION-KGE v0.1 can train a small deterministic advisory scorer from EMA graph JSONL exports.
- SEION predictions may rank candidate relationships for reviewer attention.

## Prohibited

- Do not say SEION approves readiness.
- Do not say SEION decides official compliance.
- Do not say SEION creates official evidence automatically.
- Do not say SEION replaces engineers, owner reviewers, or deterministic readiness rules.
- Do not claim Hodge, cosmology, new physics, or E8 correctness from v0.1.

## EMA Rule

PostgreSQL remains the official source of truth. The deterministic readiness engine remains the official readiness calculator.
