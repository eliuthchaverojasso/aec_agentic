# Control Plane Domain

The platform maintains four linked graphs:

- Authority graph: who can require, approve, reject, or modify work.
- Production graph: how intent becomes assets, systems, packages, tasks, and outcomes.
- Evidence graph: what information proves a state or claim.
- Economic graph: how approved progress becomes earned value, invoice support, payment, and collection.

The central object is an executable obligation. A task is only executable when its source, acceptance criteria, responsible actor, required evidence, reviewer, dependencies, and value relationship are known.
